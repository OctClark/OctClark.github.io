Refurbishing my chest of drawers

Started: August 2025

So finally finished my summer project - it can rain now, I don't mind anymore - and I have a lovely new-looking chest of drawers, without the remains of stickers my 8-year old self added.
Note to Self: Paint stripper coverage varies quite a bit - don't go for the cheapest brand, it's much less fierce.
